# Mine Defense

With your team of penguins, defend your precious resources from the zombies! 
Craft and place defences strategically to survive another night.

Created during the Hackathon X Gamejam December 2025 at Ulm University.
The topic of the Gamejam was "mistakes are permanent".

Play it on [Itch.io](https://maxatoms.itch.io/mine-defense).
